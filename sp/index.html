George

School Troubles

Just think of this as a warm up George. Your Mother will deal with you when you get home. The cane I would imagine.

George’s Mum never understood the phrase ‘this hurts me more than it hurts you.’ The caning was evidently hurting George but she didn’t feel any pain at all.

Thank you for telling me about Georges behaviour at school today, I am punishing him now. He seems quite sorry already but I guarantee he will be really sorry by the time I am finished. By the way he will find it quite uncomfortable sitting down tomorrow so could you remove his chair and allow him to stand during lessons. Yes it would be good if you could announce why this is during assembly as we wouldn’t want the other children being horrid to him.

Cought Smoking

George had been told that he should never argue about any punishments so on the day he shouted out ‘please not the hairbrush’ his mother made sure he regretted it.

The next door neighbour tells me you have been smoking at the side of the house. You may not be able to feel or see the damage it is doing to your lungs but I promise you you are gong to be able to feel and see the damage it does to your bottom.

Severe Discipline - Ordered by Mother

So look George this has just come in from your Mother. She says we should ignore the normal school limits of 24 strokes and beat you until you are truly sorry. Well Miss Wells lets give George’s mother what she asks for.

As George’s two Aunts didn’t have children they were always delighted to have George round to exercise their Maternal instincts.

Now George say sorry to your sister and her friend for how you have behaved.
